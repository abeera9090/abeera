Easy Chicken Fajitas

A classic Mexican dish, the chicken fajitas are packed with flavors and easy to make. Tender chicken strips are marinated in a blend of spices and then grilled. Serve with warm tortillas, your favourite toppings, and a side of salsa or anything of your preference.

Ingredients:

Click to View Ingredients
  • 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts (sliced into strips)
  • 1 red bell pepper (sliced)
  • 1 green bell pepper (sliced)
  • 1 yellow bell pepper (sliced)
  • 1 medium onion (sliced)
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp chili powder
  • 1 tsp cumin
  • ½ tsp garlic powder
  • ½ tsp paprika
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Juice of 1 lime
  • Flour tortillas
  • Optional: Sour cream, guacamole, shredded cheese

Cooking Instructions:

Click to View Instructions
  1. Heat olive oil in a large pan over medium-high heat.
  2. Add the sliced chicken and season with chili powder, cumin, garlic powder, paprika, salt, and pepper.
  3. Cook the chicken for about 5-7 minutes until browned and fully cooked.
  4. Remove the chicken and set it aside.
  5. In the same pan, add the sliced peppers and onion.
  6. Cook for about 5 minutes until the veggies are soft yet slightly crisp.
  7. Return the chicken to the pan and mix everything together.
  8. Squeeze fresh lime juice over the fajita mix and stir well.
  9. Warm the tortillas in a pan or microwave.
  10. Serve the chicken fajita mix in tortillas, adding your favorite toppings.
  11. Enjoy your delicious homemade Chicken Fajitas!
